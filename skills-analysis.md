## 7. Key Recommendations

### For Entry-Level Professionals

1. **Master Python + PyTorch/TensorFlow** - Non-negotiable foundations
2. **Build 3+ portfolio projects** - Deployed, documented, tested
3. **Get AWS/GCP certified** - Fast ROI, validates cloud skills
4. **Contribute to open source** - Collaboration experience

### For Mid-Level Professionals

1. **Specialize in GenAI** - Highest growth area (2025-2026)
2. **Add MLOps skills** - Kubernetes, CI/CD, monitoring
3. **Develop domain expertise** - Finance, healthcare, etc.
4. **Build leadership skills** - Team lead opportunities

### For Senior Professionals

1. **Strategic impact** - Business metrics, not just technical
2. **Architecture expertise** - System design at scale
3. **Mentorship** - Growing the next generation
4. **Industry visibility** - Speaking, writing, community
