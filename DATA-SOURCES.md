## Data Quality Assessment

### Sample Size Analysis

| Role | Sample | Confidence Level | Margin of Error |
|------|--------|------------------|-----------------|
| AI Jobs (London) | 2,395 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Very High | ±2% |
| Data Scientist | 124 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Good | ±8% |
| ML Engineer | 28 | ⭐⭐⭐ Moderate | ±18% |

**Statistical Note**: Larger sample sizes (n>100) provide high confidence. Smaller samples (n<50) should be interpreted with caution and viewed as directional rather than precise.

### Potential Biases

**✅ Strengths**:
- Real job posting data (not self-reported)
- Large sample for AI roles
- Multiple time periods for trend analysis
- Cross-validated with multiple sources

**⚠️ Limitations**:
- Job board data may miss executive/stealth hires
- Salary ranges in postings may not reflect final offers
- Geographic coverage: London-focused
- Some niche roles have small sample sizes
